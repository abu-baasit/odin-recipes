Description

A Nigerian food recipe known as pounded yam with vegetable soup

Ingredients

  • Yam.
  • Vegetables.
  • Pepper.
  • Locust beans.
  • Meat or beef.

Steps

  1. Peel yam and cut into chunks and rinse
  2. Cook till it soften
  3. Pound the yam with mortar and pestle
  4. Cook the vegetable with sauce of meat and beef
  5. Add locust beans to bring out the aroma,then allow to done
