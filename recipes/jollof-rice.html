Description

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Bell pepper
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Curry powder
  • Thyme
  • Vegetable oil
  • Bay leaves
  • chicken broth
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Blend the bell peppers,tomatoes and onions together
  2. Place a pot on cooker,add oil and sliced onion in it till its frangrance is out
  3. Add the tomato paste and other pepper to the oil
  4. Add curry,thyme and salt to the pepper and leave it to cook for like 10minutes
  5. Then add chicken broth,boiling water and rinsed rice
  6. Stir the rice for even distrbution of ingredients
  7. Then allow it to cook for close to 25 minutes
  8. Jollof rice is ready
